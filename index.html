Australia Resident Profile in 2021 Census

Population Statistic

In the 2021 Census(The lastest published data) in Australia. There are 25417345 residents live in Australia. Male and Female shows a almost balance, with female is slightly higher than male.

All states' situation are similar to this trend. The stacke bar chart on the right represents the population across different age groups by ganders. We can easily see that, the age group of 25-34 years occupies the most population, which is 3624065.

To interact with the graph, we can select the gender in any age group to visualizes that gender only, and double click anywhere around the graph to reset the filter condition.


This map visualizes the population situation by all states in Australia. The color shows the population density (Population per sqare kilometers) of the specific area. We can see that the top 3 areas are: Australian Capital Territory, New South Wales and Victoria. Most population focuses on the north-east of Australia.

By clicking on the area of any state, the map will highlight the selected state. At the same time, the stacked bar chart on the above will show the statistic of this state. To reset the filter, double click any where around the ground.

Nationality Statistic

Australia is a country contains multiple culture and oversea residents. This part will visualize the composition of the population of Australia.

The bar chart on the left shows the top 10 population countries of birth of residents in 2021 census. England, India and China are the top 3 countries, which have the population of 927490, 673352 and 549618 respectively.

We can do the same interaction to this bar chart as the previous one, which clicking a bar to select and highlight a country, and that country will be used as the filter for the line graph below.








Since we have known the top 10 Population countries of birth of residents in 2021 census. Now let's discover some trends of the arrival year of these 10 countries.

This line graph shows the arrival year of the 10 countries in different year period. It shares the same colors for countries with the bar chart above. According to this graph, we can see that there are more and more residents from India in recent 20 years. China shares the same trend. For England, it has a peak value in the period of 1961-1970, and the arrivals decreased after that.

This graph can share the same filter with the bar chart above. When select a country above, we can see the same country in this graph only, in order to analyse it more easily.
